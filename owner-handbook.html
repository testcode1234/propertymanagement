Financial Procedures

Monthly Statement Delivery

Statements are posted to your owner portal and emailed by the 15th of each month, detailing all income and expenses for the previous month.

Owner Distributions

Distributions are processed by the 10th of each month via:

  • ACH Transfer (Recommended): Direct deposit to your bank account (2-3 business days)
  • Check: Mailed to address on file (5-7 business days)
  • Hold in Reserve: Maintain balance in trust account

Reserve Accounts

We recommend maintaining a reserve balance of 1-2 months' expenses to cover:

  • Vacancy periods between tenants
  • Unexpected maintenance or repairs
  • Seasonal expenses (HVAC service, landscaping)
  • HOA fees, insurance, and property taxes

Maintenance & Repairs

Authorization Thresholds

Emergency Repairs (Under $500): Authorized immediately to protect property and tenant safety. You'll be notified within 24 hours.

Non-Emergency Repairs (Over $500): We contact you for approval with estimates and recommendations before proceeding.

Preventive Maintenance: Scheduled services (HVAC, landscaping) are pre-approved per management agreement.

Vendor Selection

We maintain a network of licensed, insured contractors providing quality service at competitive prices. You may request specific vendors, subject to our licensing and insurance requirements.

Maintenance Response Times

  • Emergency (safety/security/habitability): Within 2 hours
  • Urgent (affecting daily living): Within 24 hours
  • Routine (minor issues): Within 3-5 business days
  • Preventive/Cosmetic: Scheduled as appropriate

Property Inspections

Inspection Schedule

  • Move-In Inspection: Documented with photos before tenant occupancy
  • Quarterly Drive-By: Exterior condition check every 3 months
  • Annual Interior: Complete interior inspection once per year
  • Move-Out Inspection: Detailed condition assessment with photos
  • As-Needed: Additional inspections for specific concerns

Inspection Reports

All inspection reports include photos and detailed condition notes, accessible through your owner portal within 48 hours of completion.

Tenant Management

Tenant Selection

All applicants must meet our screening criteria:

  • Minimum credit score 650 (flexible with compensating factors)
  • Verifiable income of 3x monthly rent
  • Positive rental history and references
  • Clean criminal background (case-by-case review)
  • No evictions in past 5 years

Lease Terms

Standard residential leases are 12 months with options for shorter or longer terms based on market conditions and owner preference.

Rent Collection

Rent is due on the 1st of each month with a grace period through the 3rd. Late fees apply after the grace period per lease terms and California law.

Lease Renewals

We contact tenants 90 days before lease expiration to gauge renewal interest and discuss any rent adjustments needed to maintain market rates.

Property Marketing

Marketing Timeline

Vacant properties are typically marketed 30-45 days before availability for optimal exposure and tenant quality.

Marketing Channels

  • Zillow, Trulia, Apartments.com, and 15+ other major platforms
  • MLS (Multiple Listing Service)
  • Social media promotion
  • Yard signage
  • Professional photography and virtual tours

Pricing Strategy

We conduct comparative market analysis to determine optimal rent pricing that balances maximum income with minimal vacancy time.

Legal Compliance

Fair Housing

We strictly comply with all federal and California fair housing laws, treating all applicants and tenants fairly and equally.

Required Disclosures

All leases include required California disclosures including lead paint, mold, water conservation, and property-specific disclosures.

AB 1482 Compliance

For properties subject to California's rent cap law, we ensure annual rent increases don't exceed allowable limits (5% + CPI, max 10%).

Owner Responsibilities

As the property owner, you're responsible for:

  • Maintaining adequate property and liability insurance
  • Paying property taxes on time
  • Ensuring property meets habitability standards
  • Providing funds for approved repairs and improvements
  • Responding to approval requests in a timely manner
  • Complying with applicable laws and HOA requirements

Manager Responsibilities

Options Property Management commits to:

  • Acting as your fiduciary agent
  • Managing property professionally and diligently
  • Maintaining separate trust accounts for all funds
  • Providing timely financial reporting
  • Ensuring legal compliance
  • Coordinating all maintenance and repairs
  • Responding promptly to owner and tenant needs
  • Protecting your investment and maximizing returns

Insurance Requirements

Required Coverage

  • Dwelling Coverage: Replacement cost of structure
  • Liability Insurance: Minimum $1 million recommended
  • Loss of Rents: Covers lost income during repairs (recommended)
  • Additional Insured: Options Property Management must be listed

Emergency Procedures

Property Emergencies

For situations threatening safety, security, or habitability (flooding, fire, gas leaks, break-ins), we respond immediately 24/7 and coordinate necessary repairs.

Natural Disasters

In case of earthquakes, fires, or other natural disasters, we'll inspect your property as soon as safe and coordinate with your insurance company.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I visit my property?

Yes, as the owner you have the right to inspect your property with 24 hours notice to the tenant (per California law). Contact us to schedule a visit.

What if I want to sell?

Our agreement can be terminated with proper notice. We'll cooperate fully with showings and provide documentation to facilitate the sale.

Can I use my own contractors?

Yes, with prior approval. Your contractors must be licensed, insured, and provide competitive pricing. We coordinate all work to ensure quality and proper documentation.

What happens during vacancies?

You don't pay monthly management fees during vacancy. We handle all marketing, showings, and tenant placement at no additional monthly cost.
