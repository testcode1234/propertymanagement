Pass-Through Costs

Some expenses are paid by us on your behalf and reimbursed from your trust account at cost (no markup):

  • Maintenance & Repairs: Invoiced at contractor cost, no markup
  • Tenant Screening Reports: $40-50 per application (paid by tenant or reimbursed)
  • Lockbox/Key Services: Actual cost if needed
  • HOA Fees: Billed at actual amount
  • Property Insurance: Exact premium (if we pay on your behalf)
  • Utilities: Actual bill amounts during vacancy

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I negotiate the management fee?

We offer competitive rates based on property type, location, and services needed. Contact us to discuss a customized proposal for your specific situation.

What if my property stays vacant?

You don't pay monthly management fees during vacancy periods. We only charge management fees on rent actually collected.

Are there setup fees?

No. We don't charge onboarding or setup fees. Your only costs are the management fee once we collect rent and the leasing fee when we place a tenant.

What if I want to sell my property?

Our agreement can be terminated with 30-60 days notice. We'll cooperate fully with showings and provide all necessary documentation to buyers or their agents.
