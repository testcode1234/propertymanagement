EMAIL DISPLAY ISSUE - FIXED
============================

ISSUE:
The email address was displaying as [email protected] 
in blog post footers due to Cloudflare email obfuscation code.

ROOT CAUSE:
When HTML contains mailto: links, Cloudflare's email protection
automatically obfuscates them with JavaScript to prevent email
harvesting by bots.

SOLUTION:
Changed all blog footer emails from:
<li><a href="mailto:optionspropertymgmnt@gmail.com">...</a></li>

To plain text display:
<li>optionspropertymgmnt@gmail.com</li>

This displays the email properly as:
optionspropertymgmnt@gmail.com
