What We Inspect

Comprehensive checklist covering every aspect of your property

Interior Areas

  • Living room, bedrooms, hallways
  • Kitchen and appliances
  • Bathrooms and fixtures
  • Flooring condition (carpet, tile, hardwood)
  • Walls and ceilings (damage, stains)
  • Windows and window coverings
  • Doors and door hardware
  • Light fixtures and switches
  • Closets and storage areas

Systems & Safety

  • HVAC system operation
  • Plumbing (leaks, water pressure)
  • Electrical outlets and fixtures
  • Smoke and CO detectors
  • Water heater condition
  • Thermostat function
  • Garbage disposal operation
  • Fire extinguisher (if provided)

Exterior & Grounds

  • Roof condition and gutters
  • Siding and exterior paint
  • Driveway and walkways
  • Landscaping and lawn care
  • Sprinkler system
  • Fencing and gates
  • Garage and garage door
  • Outdoor lighting
  • Trash enclosure areas

Lease Compliance

  • Unauthorized pets or occupants
  • Unapproved modifications
  • Proper use of property
  • Cleanliness standards
  • Parking compliance
  • HOA rule adherence
  • Proper waste disposal
  • Tenant maintenance obligations
